## Interview Structure

### Stage 1: Screening (30 min)

```
GOALS:
├── Verify basic qualifications
├── Assess communication
├── Understand motivation
└── Determine culture fit signals

QUESTIONS:
1. Walk me through your background
2. Why are you interested in this role?
3. What are you looking for in your next role?
4. [Role-specific screening question]
5. What questions do you have for me?
```

### Stage 2: Skills Assessment (60 min)

```
GOALS:
├── Evaluate technical/functional skills
├── See problem-solving approach
├── Assess depth of expertise
└── Understand how they work

FORMAT OPTIONS:
├── Take-home project (2-4 hours)
├── Live coding/work session
├── Case study presentation
└── Portfolio review + deep dive
```

### Stage 3: Team Interview (45-60 min)

```
GOALS:
├── Assess team fit
├── Get multiple perspectives
├── Evaluate collaboration style
└── Answer candidate questions

PARTICIPANTS:
├── 2-3 team members
├── Mix of seniority
└── Different functions if cross-functional role
```

### Stage 4: Final/Founder (30-45 min)

```
GOALS:
├── Final culture assessment
├── Discuss vision and growth
├── Address any concerns
├── Sell the opportunity
└── Discuss compensation expectations
```

## Behavioral Interview Questions

### By Competency

**Problem Solving**

```
- Tell me about a time you faced an ambiguous problem with no clear solution
- Describe a situation where you had to make a decision with incomplete information
- What's the most complex problem you've solved? Walk me through your approach
```

**Collaboration**

```
- Tell me about a time you had a conflict with a colleague. How did you handle it?
- Describe a project where you had to work with people outside your team
- Give an example of when you had to influence someone without authority
```

**Initiative/Drive**

```
- Tell me about something you built or started without being asked
- Describe a time you went above and beyond what was expected
- What's something you've taught yourself recently?
```

**Adaptability**

```
- Tell me about a time when priorities shifted suddenly. How did you adapt?
- Describe a situation where you had to learn something new quickly
- Give an example of when you received critical feedback. What did you do?
```

**Leadership**

```
- Tell me about a time you led a team through a difficult challenge
- Describe how you've helped develop or mentor someone
- Give an example of an unpopular decision you had to make
```

## Offer & Negotiation

### Offer Letter Components

```
INCLUDE:
├── Title and reporting structure
├── Start date
├── Compensation (base, bonus structure)
├── Equity (if applicable) - # shares, vesting, strike price
├── Benefits summary
├── PTO policy
├── At-will statement
├── Contingencies (background check, etc.)
└── Expiration date for offer
```

### Negotiation Framework

```
PREPARE:
├── Know your range (min, target, max)
├── Understand what matters to candidate
├── Have trade-offs ready (salary vs equity, title, etc.)
└── Know your walk-away point

DURING NEGOTIATION:
├── Listen first, understand their priorities
├── Don't negotiate against yourself
├── Use ranges, not single numbers
├── Get to yes on non-monetary items
└── Document everything agreed
```
