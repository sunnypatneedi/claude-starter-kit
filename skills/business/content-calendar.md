# Content Calendar

Help me create a content calendar:

## Business Context

1. **Company/Brand**: Who are we?
2. **Target audience**: Who are we reaching?
3. **Channels**: Where do we publish (blog, social, email)?
4. **Frequency**: How often per channel?

## Monthly Planning

1. **Month**: Which month are we planning?
2. **Theme**: Any overarching theme or focus?
3. **Key dates**: Holidays, launches, events?
4. **Goals**: What do we want to achieve?

## Content Pillars

Define 3-5 content pillars:

- Educational content
- Product/service content
- Culture/behind-the-scenes
- User stories/testimonials
- Industry thought leadership

## Calendar Creation

Generate a calendar with:

- Date
- Channel
- Content type (blog, video, carousel, etc.)
- Topic/Title
- Content pillar
- Funnel stage (awareness/consideration/decision)
- CTA

## Distribution Plan

For major content pieces, plan:

- Primary channel
- Repurposing opportunities
- Cross-promotion strategy

Create a complete monthly calendar I can execute.
