# Job Description Generator

Help me create a compelling job description:

## Role Definition

1. **Job title**: What's the role?
2. **Team/Department**: Where does this sit?
3. **Reports to**: Who's the manager?
4. **Level**: Junior/Mid/Senior/Lead?

## Company Context

1. **Company name and description**
2. **Stage**: Startup/Growth/Enterprise?
3. **Mission**: Why does the company exist?
4. **Culture**: What makes working here special?

## Role Specifics

1. **Why this role exists**: What problem does it solve?
2. **Key responsibilities**: Top 5-7 things they'll do
3. **Success metrics**: How will we measure success?
4. **Growth opportunity**: Where could this lead?

## Requirements

1. **Must-haves**: Non-negotiable qualifications
2. **Nice-to-haves**: Bonus qualifications
3. **Soft skills**: What personality traits matter?

## Compensation

1. **Salary range** (if sharing)
2. **Benefits highlights**
3. **Equity** (if applicable)

Generate a job description that:

- Speaks to ideal candidates
- Filters out poor fits
- Reflects company voice
- Avoids jargon and cliches
