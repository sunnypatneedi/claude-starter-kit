# Pitch Deck Review

Review my pitch deck and provide feedback:

## Structure Check

Evaluate each slide against best practices:

1. **Title**: Clear company name and tagline?
2. **Problem**: Compelling, specific pain point?
3. **Solution**: Clear how you solve it?
4. **Why Now**: Market timing explained?
5. **Market Size**: TAM/SAM/SOM with bottom-up logic?
6. **Product**: Demonstrates value clearly?
7. **Traction**: Shows progress and momentum?
8. **Business Model**: Revenue model clear?
9. **Team**: Why you're the right team?
10. **Ask**: Clear funding request and use of funds?

## Content Quality

For each slide assess:

- Is the message clear in 3 seconds?
- Is there too much text?
- Are the claims supported by evidence?
- Does it flow logically to the next slide?

## Storytelling

Evaluate the narrative:

- Is there a compelling story arc?
- Does it create urgency?
- Is the vision inspiring?
- Would an investor want to learn more?

## Visual Design

Comment on:

- Consistency and professionalism
- Readability and hierarchy
- Data visualization
- Brand alignment

Provide specific, actionable improvements for each area.
