# Competitive Research

Help me research and analyze competitors:

## Define Scope

1. **Your product/service**: What do you offer?
2. **Market**: What space are you in?
3. **Competitors to analyze**: Name 3-5 competitors

## Research Framework

For each competitor, analyze:

### Company Overview

- Founded when?
- Funding/revenue (if known)
- Team size
- Headquarters

### Product Analysis

- Core offering
- Key features
- Target customer
- Pricing model

### Positioning

- How do they describe themselves?
- What's their unique angle?
- Who do they say they're for?

### Go-to-Market

- Primary acquisition channels
- Content strategy
- Sales approach

### Strengths & Weaknesses

- What do they do well?
- Where do they fall short?
- What do customers complain about?

## Competitive Matrix

Create a feature comparison matrix showing:

- Your product vs each competitor
- Key feature categories
- Pricing comparison

## Strategic Insights

Based on analysis, identify:

- Market gaps and opportunities
- Differentiation opportunities
- Threats to address
- Lessons to learn from competitors
